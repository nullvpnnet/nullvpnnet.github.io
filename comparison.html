Why NullVPN is built differently

Consumer VPNs are designed for privacy from corporations in countries where VPNs are legal and unrestricted. They are not optimised to bypass active censorship from state-level firewalls.

NullVPN is designed from the ground up for one purpose: reliable internet access in countries where the government is actively trying to block all VPN traffic. Every design decision flows from that single goal.
