Why NullVPN Works Where Others Fail

Most VPNs use protocols that Deep Packet Inspection systems can detect and block within hours. NullVPN uses a fundamentally different approach.

❌ Regular VPNs

  • Use detectable protocols (OpenVPN, IKEv2, WireGuard)
  • Run on predictable, blockable ports
  • Leave recognisable traffic fingerprints
  • IP ranges publicly listed — continuously blocklisted
  • Require app store installation

Result: Blocked in Iran, Russia, China within days

✅ NullVPN

  • Routes through the same port every bank uses
  • Traffic appears identical to HTTPS web browsing
  • Zero detectable VPN fingerprint
  • Multiple independent encryption layers
  • Auto-failover if primary route is blocked
  • No app store required — direct APK

✅ Works through corporate firewalls, national blocks, hotel Wi-Fi

The Simple Analogy

Regular VPNs are like driving a car with "VPN TRAFFIC" painted on the roof. Traffic inspection systems spot you instantly and block you.

NullVPN looks exactly like the millions of ordinary encrypted web requests passing through the same port every bank and secure site uses. Firewalls cannot block it without breaking the internet for everyone.

Why This Port Is Unblockable

The same port that handles every secure website on the internet — banking, government portals, healthcare, e-commerce. No state actor can block it without destroying their own digital infrastructure.
