Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What makes NullVPN different from NordVPN or ExpressVPN?

Consumer VPNs are built for privacy from corporations — not for bypassing active state censorship. NullVPN routes traffic through the same port that banks and governments use for HTTPS, making it impossible to block without disrupting the entire internet. We also require zero personal information and accept only anonymous crypto payments.

Is NullVPN legal?

In most countries, using a VPN is fully legal. In some countries (Iran, Russia, China), the legality of some VPN services is ambiguous or restricted. NullVPN takes no responsibility for how users choose to use the service. Users are responsible for their own compliance with local laws.

Do you keep activity logs?

No. The system is designed so no activity log is ever created. We have no idea who connects, when, or what they access. This is not just a policy — it is the architecture. There is nothing to hand over to anyone.

How do I pay? Do I need a credit card?

TON and USDT only. No credit card, no bank transfer, no PayPal. Open our Telegram Bot, pick a plan, and you’ll receive a wallet address. Send the exact amount. Your connection is provisioned automatically.

Which devices are supported?

Android is fully supported with a direct APK (no app store required). iOS, Windows, Mac, and Linux work via any compatible VPN client. Contact us via Telegram for setup instructions.

What if the connection gets blocked?

NullVPN automatically switches to a secondary route. In the rare event that a manual update is needed, we will push it automatically. You can also message us via Telegram — we respond fast.

What if this website is blocked in my country?

You can always reach us via our Telegram Bot, our Telegram Channel, or our TON Network site. All three are accessible without a VPN.

How do I install the Android app?

After payment, you receive a personal activation link via the Telegram Bot. Tap it on your Android device. The APK downloads and installs automatically — no app store needed. Tap Connect. Done.

Can I share my connection with someone else?

Each plan covers one device. For multiple people or devices, purchase additional plans. Contact us for group pricing.

What is TON?

TON (The Open Network) is a fast, low-fee blockchain originally developed by Telegram. You can buy TON on any major exchange (Binance, OKX, Bybit) or directly inside Telegram Wallet. USDT on TON is also accepted.
